Synthetic RVTools .xlsx

Builds a workbook from the template customer.xlsx: rows are filtered to match the chosen environment size, then VMs, hosts, clusters and datacenters are renamed to neutral placeholders. XXL keeps every template VM and adds synthetic clones so the total VM count is about 140% of the original (40% extra VMs).
